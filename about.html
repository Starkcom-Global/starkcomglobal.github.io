About Us

Starkcom Global is the CubeSat and ISP brand of Stark Drones. Stark Drones is a hard tech and disruptive startup founded by Andrew Magdy Kamal, a Coptic Orthodox Egyptian entrepreneur and tinkerer. Stark Drones builds a variety of technologies centered around disruptive telecom and infrastructure. 

01

From Aerostat to Gateway

Our configuration is simple, we have aerostats carrying our CubeSats that act as an entire internet balloon system. This system sends signal connectivity to our gateways. Your devices connect to these gateways. The Aerostat to gateway model is meant to be as scalable, and manufacturer-friendly as possible.

Industry Efficiency

We aim to work on industry efficiency and effective distribution so that we can focus on making a positive impact in the world. It is crucial that we create new standards within signal latency, computing, data and signal processing for the survival of this market and insuring a fair game for countries where accessibility is a crucial issue. We also want to build a global virtualized incubation hub and distributed network within the countries of Egypt, Syria and Israel to build technology that impact surrounding regions through various citizen science, sustainability and social good initiatives.
