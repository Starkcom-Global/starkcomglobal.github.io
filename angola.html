Starkcom Global:
Angola Partnership

Starkcom Global, in collaboration with Riemann Computing Inc., is engaged in providing connectivity solutions for Angola through localized venture partners. Our objective is to conduct 20 CubeSat test deployments to enhance network coverage across diverse regions in Angola. Given the country's rich biodiversity and potential in agriculture and farming, our primary use cases will focus on IoT connectivity and IoT messaging.
